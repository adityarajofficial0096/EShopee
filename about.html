Who We Are ?

As maximizers, we are each on a personal journey to excel and achieve greatness in our chosen areas of focus, whether that be in making an impact, expressing our voice, realizing our potential, developing new ideas, increasing our influence, improving our well-being, or in other areas that we care deeply about. By maximizing ourselves within our inclusive teams, we empower this e-commerce website to provide unparalleled value for our customers, stakeholders, and the planet. Our reputation is built not on the titles we hold, but rather on the impact we create. This impact is a result of working together as a team on ambitious, large-scale challenges with the goal of transforming the Indian customer experience. We believe that great ideas can come from anyone and must be supported. Our people, who are empowered by our culture of taking complete ownership, have already revolutionized India's e-commerce sector several times over! Our journey of creating India's largest unicorn startup has been marked by both successes and failures, and the invaluable lessons learned from them. This is why we prioritize calculated risk-taking, possess a strong desire to learn and adapt, and have a growth-oriented mindset woven into every aspect of our work. From launching new warehouses to improving the user experience of our mobile app, we constantly experiment, learn, and evolve to ensure continuous growth and improvement. We firmly believe that the quality of a team is only as good as the individuals who make it up. This is why we prioritize the task of finding the right people who will thrive in our high-performing, inclusive teams. We believe in giving everyone a voice at the table, and we celebrate diversity of thought, style, and action. Whether you are a category leader or a wishmaster, we are all united by our shared values of audacity, bias for action, customer-first, integrity, and inclusion.

"At the core of our organization's values is a strong commitment to our employees, placing their best interests at the forefront of our priorities."

We are all bound together and guided by our values of audacity, bias for action, customer-first, integrity and inclusion.
